About Us

Premier Security Co Ltd is a private security company duly registered and authorized to operate in Cameroon for over 25 years now. Our mission is to deliver the highest level of security solutions and provide each client with the proper guidance needed for their specific industry and individual needs. We offer vigilant armed and unarmed security guards, helpful event staff, comprehensive private investigations, insightful threat assessments, true executive protection, and the latest in camera surveillance, access control systems and monitoring solutions. We differentiate ourselves with better management and customer service, thorough guard vetting, proprietary officer training, engaged field supervisors, 24/7 guard tracking technology and a full-service infrastructure that makes working with us easy.

As one of the top security firms in the country, we have the experience and resources to deliver the right security solution. We focus on minimizing your risk and liability to give you the peace of mind you need to get back to your life or business. As a privately-owned firm, we promise effective, flexible security solutions at competitive rates with the personal attention that is often lacking with larger security companies. Decades in the business of security ensure that our clients get the right guidance for their long or short-term security challenges.
